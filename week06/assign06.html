Who We Are


Mdantsane University is the first and only university to have a township origin. The university holds itself high as one of the best universities in South Africa. No other school especially in the Eastern Cape comes close to matching its standards and achievements in such a short period of time.

To all future students, we hope you come with the right attitude of wanting to better yourself and your community. With the resources we possess, we know we can help you get there.

This website will contain more information about school activities such as Arts, Academics and Athletics. Do not hesitate to contact us for any inquery.
